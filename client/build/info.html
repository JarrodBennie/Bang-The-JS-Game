  • The Dice

    The Dice

    Hover over a dice to see what it does

    Bulls Eye 1

    Choose the player to your left or your right, and that player loses a life point.


    Bulls Eye 2

    Choose the player two places to your left or right. That player loses one life point.
    If there are only two or three players left in the game, you may use this as a one.


    Beer

    Choose any player, they gain one life point.
    You may choose yourself. You can choose to 'waste' this by healing someone who already has full health.


    Arrow

    Take one arrow per arrow rolled (ie, if you roll 3 arrows, you must take 3 arrows).
    You must resolve this immediately when rolled.
    If you take the last arrow left, The Indians attack and eachplayer loses a life point for each arrow he has.


    Dynamite

    This cannot be re-rolled! If you roll three or more dynamite you cannot reroll anymore this turn.
    If you roll three, you lose a life point. If you die as a result of three dynamites, you cannot resolve the rest of your dice.


    Gatling

    If you roll three or more Gatling, you activate the Gatling Gun and every other player loses one life point (including the Sheriff, so be careful!).
    You discard all your arrows if you use the Gatling Gun.

  • Roles

    Sheriff

    There are 2 conditions where the Sheriff can win: 1) He is the only player left standing 2) There is only the Sheriff and one or both of his deputies left.
    Choose your allies wisely, only 2 of them are there to really help you, the others may just turn around and stab you in the back!
    ~Sheriff takes 2+ life points at start of game~

    Deputies x2

    The Deputies must protect the Sheriff at all costs. It is up to them to help the Sheriff defeat all outlaws and renegades.
    The Deputy wins if the Sheriff stays alive until the end of the game. Even a 'dead' deputy still wins the game if the Sheriff is the last one left at the end.
    So Deputies, Help your Sheriff!


    Outlaws x3

    The Outlaws only goal is to kill the Sheriff. If the Sheriff is dies then the Outlaws win. Even dead outlaws win the game if the Sheriff dies. (Although dead outlaws do not win if the Sheriff dies when a renegade is still alive).
    In short, Shoot the Sheriff!

    Renegades x2

    The Renegades have possibly the hardest job. In order to win they must be standing alone with the Sheriff before they can kill him. Renegades are not a team, if they are both still standing at the end, they must take each other out before killing the Sheriff, otherwise the Outlaws win.
    So look after your Sheriff until it's just you and him, then.. Shoot him!
